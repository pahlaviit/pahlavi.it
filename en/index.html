Strategic Interests

Why Iran Matters to
Italy and Europe

Human Rights

Executions and repression are not internal affairs. Staying silent means weakening the very values upon which the European Union is founded.

Security

A democratic Iran reduces regional tensions that fuel conflicts and instability reaching as far as the Mediterranean.

Migration

Dictatorships generate uncontrolled migration flows caused by poverty and repression; democracies create stability.

Energy

A transparent and predictable energy partner would drastically reduce Italy's critical dependencies.

Terrorism

Countering ideological dictatorship means directly striking the international sources of extremism and radicalization.

Credibility

Supporting Iranian democracy demonstrates consistency between Europe's declared principles and its actual actions.
